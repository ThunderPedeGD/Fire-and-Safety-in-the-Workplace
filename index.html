Current Legislation & Framework

Under the Work Health and Safety (WHS) Act, business owners are legally obligated to ensure the health and safety of their workers. This includes providing adequate fire safety equipment, clear evacuation paths, and regular risk assessments.


Key requirements for professionals include:

  • Installation of AS 1851 compliant fire extinguishers.
  • Bi-annual fire evacuation drills.
  • Designated Fire Warden training for staff members.
